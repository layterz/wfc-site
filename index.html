Our Mission

AI needs a new medium. One that sits somewhere between film and video games that allows you to build and explore worlds that uniquely interest you in real-time.

We're starting with games, but that's just the beginning. The long term goal is to build a platform for creating arbitrary simulations for any domain. We believe this will be a safer and more flexible alternative to agent based frameworks.

  • Start with a Prompt: Simply describe what you want to see using plain English. For simple toy examples, a single prompt might be sufficient.
  • Iterate in Channels: To further develop a simulation, create a new Discord channel. Use this space to refine and iterate on your ideas, building more complex interactions and environments.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Collaborate with others in real-time, making the process of simulation creation a collective experience.
  • Watch it Unfold: As you provide instructions, watch your vision come to life in real-time as a 3D simulation built on the powerful Three.js framework.
